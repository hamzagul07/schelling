The ceiling

A flexible, cross-validated oracle scores 23.84 vs 22.99 against the compromise mean — a gap of -0.84. There is essentially no signal beyond the influence-weighted average.

Even an optimistic overfit-capable probe does not beat the mean, which is why every model tried fails to beat it.

Successor search

A pre-registered train/dev/test split, committed before any fitting. No candidate beats the compromise weighted mean on TEST.** Both point estimates are worse and both bootstrap CIs straddle zero — statistically indistinguishable from, but not better than, the mean.
