The ceiling

A deliberately flexible, cross-validated oracle scores 23.84 vs 22.99 against the compromise mean — a gap of -0.84. Even an optimistic flexible model does not beat the mean: there is essentially no signal beyond the influence-weighted average, which is why every model tried fails to beat it.
